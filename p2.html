    How to improve English Speaking and communication:-


    To improve English speaking and communication we need to learn these three vital things.

    Communication skills - ability to speak and express the language

    Team work- work with each other in your group

    Interpersonal skills- process of interacting with each other

    Adaptability- ability to adapt with different systems of the language

    How to build communication skills:

    i) Listening- The most important way to learn English is to learn to listen the language. Always remember that when you were a child you learned your mother tongue by only listening and not by grammar. By the time you were 5 years old you could speak a decent amount of words and sentences. Even at a toddler age your brain was capable enough to grasp the language just by listening. Although now we have grown-up and many developmental changes have occurred in our body, but the capacity to listen and learn have remained the same. Here in this section we teach you to practice listening. That will do the 70% of your work. Here is how to do it: -

    ii) Practice- Speaking the second most important aspect of learning English and improving communicative skill. Practice helps in two ways. (A) To make you habituated in the language. (B) To build up courage to speak. The way to do this is to pick a partner and speak with him/her daily in this language verbally .
